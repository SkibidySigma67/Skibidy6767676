Jack Hartfield Sports Club

Welcome to Jack Hartfield Sports Club

Here at Jack Hartfield Sports Club we welcome everyone from our local community. Jack Hartfield Sports Club is a welcoming community space in the heart of Acklam, built on the legacy of John Gatenby “Jack” Hatfield - the Olympic swimmer and water polo player who won three medals at the 1912 Stockholm Games and went on to become one of Teesside’s most respected sporting figures. The modern club grew from land originally connected to the old Middlesbrough Employees Sports Club, later rebuilt and officially reopened in 2007 as the Jack Hatfield Sports and Social Club, continuing his family’s long tradition of supporting local sport and community life. Today, the club offers a friendly fitness centre, lively weekly events, social activities, and flexible room‑hire facilities, all designed to give both new and long‑standing members the best possible experience!

Memberships

Becoming a member of Jack Hartfield Sports Club is simple and affordable. A one-off joining fee of £20 is required, followed by a first-year membership cost of £5, bringing the total initial payment to £25. After your first year, the annual renewal fee remains just £5.

Annual subscriptions are due on the 1st of February, although payments can be made at any time throughout the month. Please note that failure to renew within this period may result in restricted access to club facilities.

All membership payments must be made directly to Secretary Mick Mead. Payments are accepted only on Wednesday, Friday, and Saturday evenings between 8:00pm and 9:00pm.

Room-Hire

Our function room comfortably seats up to 110 guests and is available for hire from 10:00am until 11:00pm. Over the years, we’ve hosted a wide range of events including weddings, funerals, birthday celebrations, engagements, children’s parties, clairvoyant evenings, games nights, race nights, rokas, community meetings, Women’s Institute gatherings, and book launches.

Please note that the function room is unavailable on Wednesday, Friday, and Saturday evenings due to regular club activities. We are also unable to host 18th or 21st birthday parties.

Room hire is priced at £70 for non‑members, £100 for commercial bookings, and is completely free for members.

Fitness Center

The fitness room provides a friendly and supportive environment for anyone looking to begin or continue their fitness journey, welcoming both male and female members. While we do not offer personal training sessions, the room is supported by two accredited fitness instructors who are available to deliver induction sessions and offer advice and guidance on training methods and programme planning.

The room is equipped with a range of high‑quality cardiovascular and strength‑training stations, including both cable‑resistant machines and free‑weight options. This variety ensures members can enjoy a balanced and effective workout tailored to their needs.

Changing and shower facilities are available for both genders, and lockers are provided with a returnable £1.00 coin deposit for secure storage during your visit.

Although the fitness room is managed entirely by volunteers, membership fees play an important role in maintaining the equipment and contributing to the wider running of the club. Your support helps us keep the facilities safe, functional, and welcoming for everyone.
